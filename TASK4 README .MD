Social Media Do’s & Don’ts -: 
Do’s –
•	Think before you post anything 
•	Try to be polite while talking online 
•	Keep your personal info safe 
•	Share useful or positive things 
•	Check if something is true or not before sharing
 
Don’ts –
•	Don’t post anything rude or hurting others 
•	Don’t share personal details openly 
•	Don’t argue for no reason in comments 
•	Don’t share too much about your life 
•	Don’t spread fake news without checking
